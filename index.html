About Me

I am Arkaan Nabiil Heyder from Jakarta, Indonesia. I am in my fifth semester of a Cyber Security major at Bina Nusantara University. Since every application has a flaw that may be exploited, in my opinion cyber security is one of the most crucial aspects of technology. Therefore, precautions must be taken to ensure the applications security.

I appreciate learning about many Cyber Security-related topics in college, particularly penetration testing. I am currently learning more about SQL injection and XSS attacks. I also advanced to the position of becoming the leader on the majority of my academic projects, and i enjoy working both individually and collaboratively to produce the greatest results.
